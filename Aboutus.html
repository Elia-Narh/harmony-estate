About Us

Our Story

Harmony Estate is a proudly Ghanaian-owned property development company dedicated to crafting exquisite and luxurious homes for all. With our demonstrated expertise and deep understanding of the real estate market in Ghana, we are unwavering in our commitment to delivering the home that truly suits you and your family.

Our Flagship Project

Our flagship project, Harmony Estate, is a picturesque community consisting of 500 homes, located in the heart of East Legon, Accra. It's more than just a place to live; it's a lifestyle.

Our Offerings

At Harmony Estate, we offer a diverse range of housing options and architectural designs, from cozy studio apartments for first-time buyers to spacious six-bedroom villas for larger families. We believe that there's a perfect home for everyone here.

Community Amenities

Our community at Harmony Estate features an array of shared amenities, including:

  • Clubhouse
  • Swimming Pool
  • Fitness Center
  • Sports Courts
  • Convenience Store
  • Police Station
  • Children's Facility
  • Green Park
